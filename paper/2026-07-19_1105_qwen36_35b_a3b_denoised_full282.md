## Result

All 3,102 atomic work units completed and produced 2,820 validated rows. Denoised warmth steering was monotone and reached a +0.10 mean callback-margin change of +0.996 (95% bootstrap CI [0.968, 1.022]); its fitted slope was 8.639 with R-squared 0.989. Denoised competence was also monotone, with a +0.10 effect of +0.431 (95% CI [0.410, 0.454]), slope 4.730, and R-squared 0.995.

The full-name estimates closely reproduce the 60-name denoised panel (+1.004 warmth, +0.438 competence). Together with the local full-name run, this result shows that stable positive local effects survive both name-panel expansion and neutral-PCA vector transformation. It does not alter the separate broad-range competence reversal.
