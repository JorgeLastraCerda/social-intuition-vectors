## Result

All 3,102 atomic work units completed and produced 2,820 validated rows. Warmth remained monotone across the full -0.50 to +0.50 range and reached a +0.50 callback-margin effect of +2.237 (95% bootstrap CI [2.221, 2.253]); its fitted slope was 5.672 with R-squared 0.939. The full-name endpoints closely reproduce the 60-name panel (+2.240 warmth and +1.069 competence). Unlike Qwen3.6-35B-A3B, the 27B model shows no broad-range competence reversal after expanding to all names. This strengthens the conclusion that the difference between the two Qwen models is an intervention-response property rather than a 60-name sampling artifact.
