## Method

The CCU runner now accepts `local_full282`, `broad_full282`, and `denoised_local_full282` as independent tasks for either Qwen3.6 model. Each task preserves the regime identity, selects all 282 rated names, and receives its own output label, fingerprinted checkpoint directory, log, validator invocation, and success sentinel.

No scheduler or shell chaining was added. A full-name task is launched only after the corresponding model-level gate explicitly requires expansion, and every task remains independently resumable and fail-closed against output overwrite. The focused test suite and shell syntax check passed.
