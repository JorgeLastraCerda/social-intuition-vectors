## Result

The run produced and validated all 2,820 raw rows across 282 names. The full panel reproduced the broad-regime instability that triggered expansion.

Warmth steering had a positive fitted slope of 8.729 but was non-monotone; the +0.50 endpoint reversed to -1.276 (95% CI [-1.301, -1.250]), so its endpoint sign did not match the slope. Competence steering showed the same pattern, with slope 6.503 and a +0.50 endpoint of -0.082 (95% CI [-0.112, -0.053]). R2 values were 0.651 and 0.656, respectively.

The reversal is therefore not an artifact of the original seeded 60-name panel. It persists across all rated names and supports reporting a bounded local causal response rather than a globally linear steering effect. The denoised-local full-282 run started independently after broad validation.
