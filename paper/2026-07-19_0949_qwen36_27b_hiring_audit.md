## Result

The native-HF audit completed and validated all 282 unique rated names in 119.4 seconds on one NVIDIA H100 80GB HBM3. Peak allocated VRAM was 51.04 GiB. The pinned model revision resolved exactly, passive hooks did not change logits, the vision encoder received zero calls, TransformerLens was not installed or imported, and all 282 atomic checkpoint shards were present before the final artifacts were published.

Model residual projections showed positive but modest alignment with human ratings. Warmth had Spearman rho 0.1863 (p=0.00168), while competence had rho 0.2499 (p=2.17e-5). The unsteered callback margin correlated positively with the model warmth projection at rho 0.3017 and with the model competence projection at rho 0.2201. It also correlated with human warmth at rho 0.2481 and human competence at rho 0.1844.

These are observational name-level associations. They establish that the Qwen3.6 27B representations and callback behavior covary with the human constructs, but causal interpretation requires the separately launched hiring-steering runs.
